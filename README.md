Divergence is a small content mod trying to stay as vanilla as possible.

# Content

The mod currently adds:
- 3 Consumables
- 1 Enhancement
- 4 Vouchers
- 1 Joker
- 1 Sticker

# Requirements
- [Steamodded](https://github.com/Steamopollys/Steamodded)
- [Lovely](https://github.com/ethangreen-dev/lovely-injector)
